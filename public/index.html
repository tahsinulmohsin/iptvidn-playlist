🔗 Your Playlist URL

Copy this URL and paste it into any supported IPTV player. It automatically updates every 20 minutes to keep stream tokens fresh.

https://iptvidn-playlist.vercel.app/playlist.m3u8

📺 How to Play

V

VLC Media Player (PC / Mac)

1. Open VLC Media Player.
2. Go to Media > Open Network Stream... (or press Ctrl + N).
3. Paste the URL above and click Play.

T

TiviMate (Android TV / Firestick)

1. Open TiviMate and go to Settings > Playlists.
2. Select Add Playlist > M3U playlist.
3. Enter the URL above and check Include VOD if asked.
4. Click Next and let it process.

Z

Televizo (Android)

1. Open Televizo and tap the Settings (Gear) icon.
2. Tap Playlists, then tap the + button.
3. Choose New M3U playlist.
4. Name it "IPTVIDN" and paste the URL in the Playlist link field.
5. Tap the Checkmark (✓) to save.
